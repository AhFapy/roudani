Our Story The tale of Roudani Watches started when Omar from Barcelona embarked on a quest to discover the ideal fusion of luxury, religion, and spirituality in timepieces.

After some time of meticulous testing and refinement, the brand was officially unveiled in November 2023, introducing his inaugural timepiece.

The brand seamlessly melded traditional styles to curate a watch collection that is both contemporary and timeless, embodying a harmonious blend of freshness and classical elegance.

Since the launch, Roudani Watches has experienced remarkable growth, with a commitment to transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary and ensuring that each timepiece becomes a timeless everyday accessory.
